Robotics

MechaTron

Build your robot and choose your arena — Fight (destroy the opponent) or Race (beat the clock).

Categories: Fighting & Racing
Fighting Team: Up to 5 members
Racing Team: Up to 4 members
Self-funded: Yes — no funds from school
Overview

Participants build their own robots and compete in one of two categories: arena fighting or time-trial racing. Individual participation is also allowed in both categories.

Category A — Fighting
  • Maximum 5 participants per team (individual allowed).
  • Arena has two concentric circles — inner for fighting, outer for controllers.
  • Robot dimensions: must not exceed 12×12×12 inches.
  • No projectile weapons, flamethrowers, acid throwers, or sharp structures.
  • A robot is eliminated if it exits the inner circle for a set time, or is incapacitated.
  • Pairings decided by the TechQuest Committee.
Category B — Racing
  • Maximum 4 participants per team (individual allowed).
  • Track minimum width: 10 inches. Robot dimensions: must not exceed 10×10×10 inches.
  • Winner determined by fastest track completion time.
  • Incapacitated robots restart from the nearest checkpoint; recovery time is counted.
  • No mechanisms that sabotage the track or other robots.
General Rules
  • Transport, placement, and care of the robot is entirely the team's responsibility.
  • No funds will be provided by the TechQuest Committee or the school.
  • Any damage before or after the competition is not the school's responsibility.
  • Passive-aggressive behaviour, obscene designs, or targeting any community — immediate disqualification.
