Public Speaking

Do DubDub

Watch a silent video, write a script in 7 minutes, then perform your live voiceover when it replays.

Team Size: 2–4 members
Rounds: 2
Mode: Team
Overview

A video clip is played without audio. Teams prepare a dubbing script, then perform their voiceover live during the replay. Round 2 adds a genre twist — teams must dub in an assigned style.

Round Structure
Round 1
Soundless Script Showdown
  • All teams participate with shorter video clips.
  • 7 minutes preparation time after silent viewing.
  • Best-performing teams advance to the final.
Round 2 — Finals
Dub-Off: The Genre Gauntlet
  • Each team is assigned a genre (comedy, horror, sci-fi, musical, action, historical, etc.).
  • Longer or more complex video clips used.
  • 5 minutes preparation time.
  • Dialogue, tone, and character voices must align with the assigned genre.
  • Top 3 teams declared winners.
Rules
  • Minimum 2 members, maximum 4 per team.
  • No abusive or inappropriate language — immediate disqualification.
  • All content must be school-appropriate and respectful.
  • Creative accents and character voices are strongly encouraged.
Scoring
Creativity & Originality10 pts
Voice Synchronization10 pts
Humour / Entertainment Value10 pts
Team Coordination5 pts
Genre Relevance (Round 2 only)20 pts
