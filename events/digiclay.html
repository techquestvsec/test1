Media & Arts

DigiClay

Model and animate an original 3D sequence in Blender — realism, originality, and technical rigging all factor into the score.

Software: Blender
Mode: Individual / Team
Topic: Revealed on event day
Overview

Participants create an original 3D animation using Blender. Topic is given on the day. All models must be original — pre-built assets are not allowed.

Judging Criteria
  • Realism — quality of lighting, textures, and materials.
  • Originality — unique models and concept.
  • Technical Skill — rigging, animation curves, and scene composition.
