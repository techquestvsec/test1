Design

Slide Master

Design a PowerPoint presentation on a given topic in under an hour — layout, clarity, relevance, and delivery are all scored.

Software: PowerPoint
Mode: Individual
Classes: 5th & 6th (Junior event)
Slides: 5–8 per presentation
Overview

Participants create a PowerPoint presentation on a provided topic within a time limit. A folder with optional text and images is available on each system. Use, modify, or ignore the provided content — your call.

Round Structure
Round 1 — Preliminary (1 Hour)
Creative Deck
  • 12 shortlisted students participate.
  • Topic announced 10 minutes before the round begins.
  • No internet access. Pre-loaded folder with 7–8 relevant images available.
  • Minimum 5, maximum 8 slides.
  • Top 6 advance to Round 2.
Round 2 — Final (1 Hour)
Final Showdown
  • Top 6 students from Round 1 compete.
  • New topic given 5 minutes before round begins.
  • New folder with fresh images available.
  • Each participant presents their slides to judges (2–3 minutes).
Rules
  • No internet access during any round.
  • No external storage devices or pre-prepared files allowed.
  • External help results in immediate disqualification.
  • Provided text and images are optional — you may replace or edit them.
Scoring
Creativity & Visual Appeal10 pts
Relevance to Topic10 pts
Slide Formatting & Layout10 pts
Presentation Clarity10 pts
Overall Impact10 pts
Total50 pts
