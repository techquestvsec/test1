Scavenger Hunt

A hybrid digital-physical quest — solve layered clues, complete timed challenges, solo or as a team.

Mode: Solo / Team
Tasks: Online, offline, or mixed
Judged on: Completion & Speed
Overview

Participants solve a series of clues and complete challenges within set time limits. Tasks may span digital platforms, physical locations, or a combination of both.

Rules
  • Groups or solo participation allowed.
  • Clues and tasks are revealed progressively during the event.
  • All challenges must be completed within their respective time limits.
Judging Criteria
  • Completion — number of challenges successfully finished.
  • Speed — total time taken to complete all tasks.
