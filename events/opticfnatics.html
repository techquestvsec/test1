OpTic Fnatics

Two-day Minecraft tournament — Day 1: Build Battle, Day 2: Manhunt. Last builder standing wins.

Game: Minecraft
Mode: Individual
Duration: 2 Days
Starting Players: 20
Overview

A two-day Minecraft event with 20 players. Day 1 focuses on building; Day 2 is a high-stakes manhunt. Top 10 builders from Day 1 advance.

Day 1 — Build Battle
Part A
Mechanical Build (Redstone)
Build a functional redstone contraption. Judged on complexity and functionality.
Part B
Aesthetic Build
Build the most visually impressive structure. Judged on creativity and design.

Event is 4 hours total: 1h 45min per build, 30min for judging. 10 players are eliminated; 10 advance to Day 2.

Day 2 — Manhunt
2 Rounds
Last Man Standing
  • 10-minute buffer period before hunters start.
  • Collect resources while evading hunters.
  • You can eliminate other players (in-game).
  • Eliminated players can respawn unless killed by a hunter.
  • Last player standing wins.
Gaming Rules
  • Strictly formal language only — no informal or abusive speech.
  • Griefing is allowed.
  • Tampering with other players' physical equipment is not allowed.
  • Spawn soft-locking is permitted.
  • Defensive mechanisms for escaping hunters are allowed.
