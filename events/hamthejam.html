Public Speaking

Ham The Jam

Impromptu speaking under live fire — pick a chit, speak on the spot, and outlast every interjection.

Rounds: 2 (HAM + JAM)
Mode: Individual
Classes: 9–12
Overview

Two rounds of impromptu speaking. In HAM (Half A Minute) you speak for 30 seconds; in JAM (Just A Minute) you speak for 60. Competitors can interject if you fumble, pause, or drift off-topic — and take your spot.

HAM — Half a Minute
Round 1 — MIC DROP MAYHEM
Classes 9–12
  • Pick a chit and speak on the topic for 30 seconds.
  • Others interject (by saying "HAM") if you fumble, take an unnecessary pause, or go off-topic.
  • The interjector takes over for the remaining time.
  • Reason for interjection must be stated; invalid reason = −5 pts.
  • Minimum 5, maximum 10 students selected for JAM.
Speaking for 15 seconds10 pts
Speaking for 30 seconds25 pts

Concluding speech+10 pts

Round 2 — SPEAK FREAK
Classes 9–12

  • Same interjection rules apply.
  • Top 3 students declared winners.
