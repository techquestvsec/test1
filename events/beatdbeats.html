Beat D Beats

Remix and mash-up provided audio tracks using Audacity, FL Studio, or Acid Pro into a polished original composition.

Software: Audacity / FL Studio / Acid Pro
Mode: Individual / Team
Tracks: Provided on event day
Overview

Participants mix and remix provided audio tracks to create an original musical arrangement. Bring your own wired headphones or earphones — wireless are not permitted.

Rules
  • Participants must bring wired headphones or wired earphones.
  • Only the approved software may be used: Audacity, Acid Pro, FL Studio.
  • All source audio tracks are provided — no external audio imports.
  • Selection via pre-elimination test conducted by event host.
Judging Criteria
  • Creativity — originality of the remix.
  • Technical Editing — skill in mixing, layering, and mastering.
  • Overall Impact — how engaging the final track sounds.
